📌 What You're Looking At

This is a working demo of an AI agent system — one for the Chief's office and one for each of the 7 councillors. Each agent handles the repetitive administrative work for their portfolio: tracking deadlines, drafting documents, monitoring grants, preparing meeting materials, and organizing information. Staff always reviews and approves everything. The agents just do the first pass — like having a dedicated assistant for every portfolio, working 24/7.

👤 Who's Building This

Dallas D Moosehunter is a band member of Sturgeon Lake First Nation. This isn't an outside vendor — it's a community member building AI tools for their own community. All technical work is handled remotely. Updates, monitoring, and support are included. The goal: make SLFN a model for how First Nations use AI to strengthen self-governance.
